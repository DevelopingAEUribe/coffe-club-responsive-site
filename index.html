Mid-City Cultural Coffee Club

Meet up. Drink Coffee. Converse.

Come join Mid-City's vibrant coffee community whose focus is being informed about coffee from Africa and Latin Americ and most importantly tasting coffee beans from beautiful countries around the world!

Mid-City CVultural Coffee Club is a free MeetUp group sponsored by Hilltop Coffee.

"I create availability in my schedule every week to attend these events. I reccomend these events to whoever loves coffee and wants to be informed on where their beverages are sourced from!"

-Brandon S. - Restaurant owner from Los Angeles

Come join the brew!

We meet every morning in Mid-City at 9am!
